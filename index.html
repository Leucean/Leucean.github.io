About Me!


Heya! I'm Leucean (Ly tʃe ən), a Clouded Leopard and an occasional lynx from the tropics!


Art by Lulukaju, 2023


My pronouns are He/They, and I'm a Panromantic Demisexual dude!


Art by Leucean, 2024.


I'm a university student from Singapore, and Toronto is my adopted home!


I have many, many hobbies and I love to talk about them! From the nerdiest of nerdy stuff like D&D, to the jock-iest sportsball stuff like Football! You can find the whole list here!


I enjoy a good hike! If I'm ever in your area and you know an amazing hiking trail to scamper down, hit me up!


Sometimes, I make music. Sometimes, I make art. Most of the time, you will find me singing (badly) to songs I really enjoy. I occasionally commission art, and you can find them on my FA!


I'm also a massive Strategy Game nerd, so if you play games like Civilization, Humankind, Stellaris, or even obscure economic simulators like X4: Foundations and want to play multiplayer or discuss it, you should add me on Steam!


So yeah, if you see me, feel free to say hi and ask for a hug! If you give me Boba Tea, I will be eternally grateful! That's pretty much it! Thanks for reading my page!
