About Me!


Heya! I'm Leucean, a Clouded Leopard from the tropics!

Art by Lulukaju, 2023


My pronouns are He/They, and I'm a Panromantic Demisexual dude!


Art by Leucean, 2024.


I'm currently a university student based in Singapore. From August 2024 to January 2025, you can find me gallivanting around North America, mostly in Toronto, Canada! If you're from Toronto or York University, send me a message!


I have many, many hobbies and I love to talk about them! From the nerdiest of nerdy stuff like D&D, to the jock-iest sportsball stuff like Football! You can find the whole list here!


I enjoy a good hike! If I'm ever in your area and you know an amazing hiking trail to scamper down, hit me up!


Sometimes, I make music. Sometimes, I make art. Most of the time, you will find me singing (badly) to songs I really enjoy.


I'm also a massive Strategy Games nerd, so if you play games like Civilization, Humankind, Stellaris, or even obscure economic simulators like X4: Foundations and want to play multiplayer or discuss it, you should add me on Steam!


So yeah, if you see me in real life, feel free to say hi and ask for a hug! If you give me Boba tea, I will be eternally grateful! That's pretty much it, thanks for reading my page!
