I'm pretty easy to find! Look for the kitty with zoomies! Whenever I'm partialling, I'm usually wearing a flannel shirt. I'm basically always looking for an excuse to wear a flannel shirt. Otherwise, look for an average height dude with "Leucean" on their badge!

Dos and Do Nots

Do:

  • Say hi to me
  • Ask me for a hug
  • Ask me to hang out
  • Ask me for a photo

Do NOT:

  • Touch me without consent
  • Assume I can hear you
  • Grab my tail

Please keep in mind that costume is NOT consent.

When wearing a fursuit, your vision and hearing will be VERY impaired. If you need to catch my attention, you should speak near my fursuit's cheek or ears! If you want me to see you, try to be slightly off-centre of my muzzle! When you stand directly in front of me, most of the time I can't see you!
