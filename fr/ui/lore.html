Leucean Cahatane-Leure est né en l'an 628 dans la ville de Taurangit à Felamoatu. Iel est à la fois Tangan Felas et Serbleux. Son père est un descendant de la lignée légendaire des Cahatane, tandis que sa mère est comme diplomate serbleuquer.

Son nom signifie « né de la rivière » en serbleuquois, sa langue maternelle. Iel n'est pas né de la rivière, mais iel est né au bord de l'océan.

Leucean est un navigateur passionné. Iel passe son temps à explorer les villes et la nature, observant les gens et les lieux. Le Wharote Langkit, le plus grand observatoire, était son lieu préféré pour rester. Les heures passèrent tandis que Leucean était étendu là.

Leucean was always drawn to the ocean. Whenever the water called, they would be in a Wakerang, sailing. After Leucean's coming of age, they chose to join their local pearl-diving guild instead of following in their parents' diplomatic footsteps - much to their parents' chagrin.

It was on a pearl-diving expedition that Leucean found their treasured moonstone. While underwater off the coast of Palau Laiwani, a sudden storm formed over him. When they attempted to resurface with the oysters they had gathered, the stormy waves swept them away from their Wakerang.

They woke up on a beach, somehow alive despite drowning. The sack of the oysters they had gathered was empty - save for the glittering moonstone at the bottom. They had somehow floated to Palu Taiwiti, the westernmost island governed by Felamoatu.

They eventually returned to Taurangit after their near death, albeit a bit shaken. However, the incident renewed their desire to explore - they wanted to see as much of the world before they grew old and unable to.

Driven by their desire for adventure and a renewed purpose, Leucean set out to explore the rest of the world.

Leucean, as seen in DND

Leucean is a Storm Sorcerer with the Chaotic Good alignment and the Sailor background.

Their stats spread prioritises Charisma and Dexterity. They have a moonstone spellcasting focus - found next to them after they were mysteriously rescued during a pearl-diving accident in a storm. It is attached to a golden pendant they wear around their neck.
