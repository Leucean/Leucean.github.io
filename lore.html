Leucean's Lore

Leucean was born in the year of 628, in the city of Taurangit in Felamoatu. He has mixed heritage, born of Felas and Serbluequer blood. Their father is a descendent of the great Cahatane lineage, while their mother was a Serbleuquer envoy to the isles.


Born and raised in Taurangit, he is accustomed to the warm, humid jungle of his home island.


He was raised in both cultures, embracing the strong community bonds of his homeland, and the individualism of his distant brethren.

Leucean, as seen in DND

Leucean is a Storm Sorcerer Sorcerer with the Chaotic Good alignment and the Sailor background. His stats spread prioritises Charisma and Dexterity. He has a moonstone spellcasting focus - found while pearl-diving, which is a sign of favour from the Goddess of Oceans, Kaila. It is attached to a golden pendant he wears around his neck.
