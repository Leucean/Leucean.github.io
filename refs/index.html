Leucean's Reference

Leucean (they/them) is a masculine non-binary clouded leopard. They can be depicted with a range of body types from twink to average, but always is androgynous.

Aesthetics and Vibes
Warm, earthy colours, loose silk clothing or flannel clothing (for modern settings), tropical and oceanic, relaxed and cozy.
Personality
A little bit chaotic, a little bit neurotic, very hyperactive, but always optimistic and kind-hearted. Leucean's personality is essentially "cat that scampers from point to point". They talk very musically and expressively with an animated and high pitched voice. They are very direct and always speak their mind.

Leucean is always seeking adventure, and they enjoy exploring the world. Their goal in life is understand the cultures and perspectives of the world. They hope that their diverse experiences can better the lives of their community, family and friends.
